Than Sadet Waterfall

■ Verify before you go
  • 1Haad Rin Queen status — aggregators show it running (17:00 & 18:30 from Big Buddha pier) but DirectFerries disputes it. Call ☎ +66 77 484 668 or ask at the pier Friday. Plan B: Lomprayah from its own Bangrak pier, or Monday 08:00.This is the plan's only single-point dependency.
  • 2Book Bangkok Airways now — it's a monopoly route in peak European-holiday season; the ฿2,500–4,500 range hardens the longer you wait.
  • 3Tournament schedule from Padel Tropical — the itinerary assumes a ~15:30 Sunday finish; anything past ~17:45 shifts the crossing to Monday 08:00 (budget already absorbs this).
  • 4Tide table for Monday 3 Aug — Koh Ma snorkeling wants high tide; August middays run shallow.
  • 5Full Moon Party date — scheduled 31 Jul but flagged as movable around Buddhist Lent; re-check ~25 Jul. It only affects Friday-night noise and Sunday ferry crowds, not your plan.
  • 6Hotel rate for the exact nights — Bangrak Pier Bungalow is already sold out for these dates; Secret Garden (12 bungalows only) will go next. Lock it on Booking/Agoda now, with Samui Mermaid as the roomier 77-room fallback.

Weather, early August: green season at its gentlest — ~32°C, sunny mornings, short sharp late-afternoon showers, calm seas (the Gulf's rough months are Oct–Dec). Three of Padel Tropical's five courts are covered, so rain won't stop play. Plan outdoor Phangan things for mornings.
