Universal Studios Singapore Full Day
Morning

Arrive 09:45 for the 10:00 open — straight to Sci-Fi City and Minion Land before queues build. Grab to Sentosa ฿390–650 (S$15–25) or MRT + Sentosa Express ฿104 pp (S$4).

Afternoon

Full park day (typical close 20:00). Late July isn't a Singapore school holiday — Monday crowds are light, so skip the Express Pass and save ~฿9,400 (S$360). Indoor rides when the 1pm shower passes through. tickets ~฿7,550 family (S$290)

Evening

Dinner at VivoCity on the way home, or collapse gratefully at the hotel.

Singapore Zoo & Chinatown optional padel
Morning

Lazy morning — sleep in and hit the hotel pool before the zoo. Optional padel: 90 minutes at Pop Padel, 8A Lengkok Bahru, Redhill — 10 min from the hotel, covered courts, book via the Pop Padel app or WhatsApp +65 8024 5700. ฿4,060 / 90 min (S$156)

Singapore Zoo with unlimited tram — arrive by 13:30, last entry 17:00. ฿1,270 adult / ฿880 child (S$49 / 34) Optional upgrade: Mandai Xperience Pass adds Night Safari + River Wonders ฿2,470 / ฿1,690 (S$95 / 65).

Evening

Chinatown wander, then 🍕 dinner at ANTO Pizza e Aperitivi (2 Jiak Chuan Rd, Tanjong Pagar) — Singapore's best pizzeria, #15 in Asia-Pacific on 50 Top Pizza 2026. ~฿620–1,090 per pizza (S$24–42++), daily to 23:00 — reserve ahead (~80 seats, tel 9689 6869). Hawker fallback: Maxwell Food Centre (Tian Tian chicken rice) — or stay north for Night Safari if you took the pass.
